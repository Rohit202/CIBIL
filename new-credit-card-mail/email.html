Did you know?

Maintaining a good credit utilization ratio has a positive impact on your CIBIL Score.

What is credit utilization ratio?

Credit utilization measures the balances you owe on your credit cards relative to the cards credit limits.

What is a good credit utilization ratio?

You should keep your credit utilization ratio less than 30%. That applies to each individual card and your total credit utilization ratio.

How to maintain a good credit utilization ratio?

  • Pay your credit card bills in full and on time.
  • Decrease your spending.
  • Ask for a higher credit limit.
  • Apply for an additional credit card to increase your total credit limit.
  • Don’t close unused cards.
